Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture until the spaghetti is coated.
  5. Serve immediately and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
